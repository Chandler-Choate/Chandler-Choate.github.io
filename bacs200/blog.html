What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital form of currency with each transaction being monitored by a software called cryptography or “secret writing”. This software allows for secure transactions that only the sender and receiver can access, and verifies that the transaction was complete; instead of the traditional banking system.

This new form of currency can affect our society, socially, economically, and politically.
