Why Choose Big Bones Canine?

No Dogs Left Behind

Are you looking to adopt a furry friend? Well look no farther than Big Bones Canine Rescue! It brings us joy getting to help find you the perfect dog that will fit right in with your family.

When choosing a dog it is important to remember that you are everything to them, and they were placed on this Earth to bring happiness to anyone that comes in contact with them. No matter the stage of a dog's life, Big Bones Canine Rescue is here to help each dog find their forever family.

Forever Homes

As a no kill shelter, Big Bones Canine strives to provide the best care for our dogs until they can find their forever families. What is a forever home? A forever home is just as it sounds, it's a family that will unconditionally love their dog, just as their dog will do for them.

Being a forever family takes commitment, in the fun times and the not so fun, like bath time. Meaning you should make sure you are prepared, before applying to adopt. Once you are ready we know we can help you find the perfect companion to complete your family.

Stay in Touch

Once you adopt your furry friend, we want you to continuously show us how you spoil them. Whether you are taking them to the park, on a cross country road trip, or simply to get a pup cup from their favorite coffee shop, make sure to show us. Tag us on Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter and use our hashtag, #bigbonescaninerescue, so we can join in on the fun!

Remember adopting a dog won't only change their life for the better, but yours as well. For a dog, you are their whole world.

How to Help

Not ready to adopt? No worries, there are other ways you can help make an impact on dogs' lives. The easiest way is to donate. Each donation is used to help pay for food, toys, shelter, and surgeries. As a nonprofit, donations are how we support each dog. Donate via Paypal or Venmo @bigbonescaninerescue.

If you feel inclined to do more, you can sponsor a Puppy Day Out! which allows us to spoil our dogs by taking them to go do special activities, such as one-on-one activities with a volunteer. Each dog deserves to live a happy life, and we want just that.

Make sure to follow us on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter to stay up to date on all our adoptable dogs and our lastest events.
